## SEQUENCE STRATEGY NOTES

- **Thread to content:** This is Day 5 of the 7-day launch arc. Days 1-4 have established the problem, shared the personal story, hinted at the solution, and provided social proof. Today's reveal lands on top of 4 days of priming -- the audience should already understand why this matters.
- **Reply handling:** Every DM gets a personal response within 2 hours. Common responses:
  - "How do I apply?" -> Direct them to the link sticker on Slide 8
  - "How much is it?" -> Share the price with the value context: "The investment is $3,000 for the full 12 weeks. That includes [brief value recap]. Want me to walk you through it?"
  - "Is this right for me?" -> Ask 2-3 qualifying questions to assess fit
- **Data to collect:** Track link clicks from the sticker. Monitor DM volume hour by hour. Note which slide people screenshot (indicates what resonated most).
- **Follow-up sequence:**
  - Day 6: Objection Breaking sequence (addresses the #1 objection from DMs received today)
  - Day 7: Final CTA with urgency + deadline + strongest testimonial
- **Parallel content today:**
  - Instagram Reel: Transformation reel featuring a client who mirrors the ICP
  - Twitter: Announcement thread with full program breakdown
  - LinkedIn: Case study post with soft CTA
  - YouTube: Re-share VSSL with new intro card

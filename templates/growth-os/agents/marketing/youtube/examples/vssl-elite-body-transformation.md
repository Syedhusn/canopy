With planned refeeds. With deload weeks built in. With sleep and stress as actual tracked variables, not afterthoughts.

Over the next 6 months, I went from 185 to 172 -- but more importantly, I gained muscle while losing fat. My body composition transformed in a way that 4 years of "eat less, move more" had never achieved.

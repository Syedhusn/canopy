## VISUAL/CREATIVE DIRECTION

- **Format:** 9:16 (Reels/Stories placement primary) + 4:5 (Feed placement)
- **Visual style:** Creator talking head. Natural lighting. Gym, kitchen, or home office. Not studio-produced -- should feel like organic content the viewer would see in their feed.
- **Key visuals:** Creator face (primary), brief client before/after (5 sec max), phone screen showing the assessment quiz
- **Text overlay:** Bold, centered, 5-7 words max per overlay. White text with slight shadow on varied backgrounds. Overlays change every 5-8 seconds to maintain visual dynamism.
- **Thumbnail (for feed placement):** Creator face with "Your Metabolism Is ADAPTED" text overlay. Surprised or frustrated expression.
- **Brand elements:** No logo until final CTA frame. Minimal branding -- ad should feel native.
- **Music/audio:** No music. Creator voice only. Feels personal and direct.

---

## TARGETING RECOMMENDATIONS

- **Custom audience:** None (cold traffic)
- **Lookalike source:** 1% lookalike based on existing email list or website visitors (if available). If not available, build from video view audiences after first week.
- **Interest targeting:**
  - Primary: Fitness, Weight loss, Bodybuilding, CrossFit, Personal training
  - Layered with: Online coaching, Fitness apps (MyFitnessPal, Noom competitors), Health and wellness
- **Demographic:** Ages 25-45, all genders
- **Exclusions:**
  - Existing email subscribers
  - People who already took the assessment
  - People who purchased (customers)
- **Placement:** Instagram Reels (primary), Instagram Feed, Facebook Feed, Facebook Reels. Exclude Audience Network and Messenger.

## TESTING PLAN

- **Phase 1 (Days 1-3):** Test 5 hook variants with Body Variant 1 + CTA A
  - Budget: $30/day per variant ($150/day total)
  - Success metric: CTR > 1.5% and CPL (cost per lead) < $8
  - Kill metric: CTR < 0.8% after 48 hours

- **Phase 2 (Days 4-6):** Winning hook + test Body Variant 1 vs. Body Variant 2
  - Budget: $50/day per variant ($100/day total)
  - Success metric: CPL < $6

- **Phase 3 (Days 7-9):** Winning hook + winning body + test 3 CTAs
  - Budget: $50/day per variant ($150/day total)
  - Success metric: CPL < $5

- **Phase 4 (Day 10+):** Scale winner
  - Increase budget 20% every 3 days if CPL holds
  - Scale trigger: CPL < $5 sustained for 5 days
  - Kill trigger: CPL > $10 for 48 hours

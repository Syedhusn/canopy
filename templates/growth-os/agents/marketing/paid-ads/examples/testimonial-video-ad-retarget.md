## VISUAL/CREATIVE DIRECTION

- **Format:** 9:16 (primary for Stories/Reels) + 1:1 (Feed)
- **Visual style:** Sarah filmed in her home, natural lighting. NOT studio. Should feel like a friend sharing her experience on a video call. Creator segments filmed in their usual content setting.
- **Key visuals:** Sarah's face (trust), before/after photos (proof), creator's face (credibility on close)
- **Text overlays:** Key metrics appear as overlays during relevant sections. Keep on screen for 3-5 seconds. Bold, high-contrast.
- **Before/after:** Side-by-side for 3-4 seconds. Same lighting, same pose, clear difference. Dates visible.
- **Brand elements:** Minimal. Program name appears only at CTA. No logo watermark.
- **Audio:** Sarah's voice + creator's voice. No background music (authenticity > production value).
- **Subtitles:** Full subtitles throughout (80%+ of mobile viewers watch without sound).

## TARGETING RECOMMENDATIONS

- **Custom audience:**
  1. Metabolic Assessment completers (lead magnet optins) -- last 60 days
  2. VSSL viewers who watched 50%+ -- last 30 days
  3. Website visitors (pricing/application page) -- last 14 days
  4. Instagram/Facebook engagers (high intent: saves, shares, DMs) -- last 30 days
- **Lookalike source:** 1% lookalike of email subscribers who opened 3+ emails (warm behavior signal)
- **Exclusions:**
  - Existing customers/clients
  - People who already applied (regardless of status)
  - People who were rejected in the last 90 days
- **Placement:** Instagram Feed, Instagram Stories, Facebook Feed. Manual placement -- no Audience Network for BOFU.

## TESTING PLAN

- **Phase 1 (Days 1-3):** Test 3 hook variants with Body Variant 1 + CTA A
  - Budget: $40/day per variant ($120/day total)
  - Success metric: CPA (cost per application) < $50
  - Kill metric: CPA > $100 after 72 hours

- **Phase 2 (Days 4-6):** Winning hook + test Body Variant 1 vs. Variant 2
  - Budget: $60/day per variant ($120/day total)
  - Success metric: CPA < $40

- **Phase 3 (Days 7-9):** Winning combination + test 3 CTAs
  - Budget: $60/day per variant ($180/day total)
  - Success metric: CPA < $35

- **Phase 4 (Day 10+):** Scale winner
  - Scale trigger: CPA < $35 sustained for 5 days
  - Kill trigger: CPA > $75 for 48 hours
  - Budget increase: 20% every 3 days while KPIs hold

## PERFORMANCE BENCHMARKS

- Target CPM: < $25 (warm audience, higher quality = higher CPM is acceptable)
- Target CTR: > 3% (retarget should outperform cold)
- Target CPC: < $3.50
- Target CPA (application): < $35
- Target Application-to-Call Rate: > 60%
- Target Call-to-Close Rate: > 30%
- Implied ROAS: At $35 CPA, 60% app-to-call, 30% close rate on $3,000 offer = $3,000 / ($35 / 0.18) = 15.4:1 ROAS
- **Unit economics validation:** Each application costs ~$35. Each 5.5 applications yields 1 client at $3,000. Customer acquisition cost: ~$194. LTV:CAC ratio: 15.4:1 (exceeds 3:1 threshold).

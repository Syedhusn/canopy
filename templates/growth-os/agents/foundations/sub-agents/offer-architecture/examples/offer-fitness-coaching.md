## 11. Economics Validation

| Metric | Value | Basis |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Blended LTV** | $4,565 | (35% x $697) + (50% x $3,500) + (15% x $12,000) = $244 + $1,750 + $1,800 = $3,794. Plus 15% upsell from Coached to Elite over 12 months: 0.15 x $8,500 additional = $1,275 x 0.50 probability = $638. Subtotal: $4,432. Adjusted for 3% refund rate: $4,299. Plus 5% rebuy rate (next cohort/continuation): +$266. Net LTV: $4,565. |
| **Estimated CAC** | $650 | Warm traffic (email, social, podcast): $200-$400. Cold traffic (Meta, YouTube ads): $800-$1,200. Blended at 60/40 warm/cold: $440 warm + $960 cold / 2 = $650 blended. |
| **LTV:CAC** | 7.0:1 | $4,565 / $650 = 7.0. Exceeds 3.0 minimum substantially. |
| **Gross Margin** | 82% | Revenue: $4,565. COGS per customer: payment processing (3%) = $137, platform fees (2%) = $91, coach time (flagship: 12 hours x $50 = $600 allocated across 20 clients = $30/client blended). Total COGS: ~$820 blended. Margin: ($4,565 - $820) / $4,565 = 82%. |
| **Break-Even** | 8 clients or Month 2 | Fixed costs: ~$3,000/month (platform, tools, marketing infrastructure, contractor coach retainer). At blended revenue of $3,794/client (before upsell), break-even at ~8 clients for the first cohort. Achievable in the first cohort with a 200-person email list at 4% conversion. |
| **Payback Period** | < 1 month | At $650 CAC and $3,794 average first purchase, the CAC is recovered immediately at purchase. Payback period is effectively zero for this offer structure (one-time payment, not subscription). |

**Stress Test:**
- If conversion drops 50%: Break-even extends to 16 clients (still achievable with paid traffic supplementing warm list)
- If refund rate doubles to 6%: LTV drops to ~$4,400. LTV:CAC still 6.8:1. Still viable.
- If CAC doubles to $1,300: LTV:CAC drops to 3.5:1. Still above 3.0 minimum but would require optimization.
- **Worst case (all three):** LTV:CAC = ~2.5:1. Below threshold. Would need to either reduce CAC (more warm traffic development) or increase upsell rate.

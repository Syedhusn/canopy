# SOP: Inbound Lead-to-Close Sales Process

**Category:** Sales Process
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** Alex Martinez (Setter) / Marcus Rivera (Closer)
**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026
**Review Cadence:** Monthly
**Estimated Time:** 5 minutes (triage) + 10 minutes (qualification) + 45 minutes (sales call) per lead

---

## Purpose

Convert inbound leads into qualified sales calls and closed deals for the FitFoundation 12-Week Transformation Program ($3,000). This SOP ensures every lead receives a response within 5 minutes, is systematically qualified using the BANT framework, and is either booked for a closer call or routed to the appropriate nurture sequence. No lead falls through the cracks.

---

## Scope

**Covers:** All inbound leads from website forms, DMs (Instagram and Facebook), ad responses, referrals, and webinar registrations.

**Does NOT cover:** Outbound cold prospecting (see SOP: Outbound SDR Process), existing client upsells (see SOP: Client Expansion), or partnership/affiliate inquiries (escalate to Marcus directly).

## Procedure

### Step 1: Lead Triage

**Tool:** GoHighLevel > Contacts > Smart List: "New Leads Today"
**Time:** 2 minutes per lead
**When:** Check every 30 minutes during business hours (9am-6pm EST) OR immediately when push notification arrives

**Action:** Open the lead's contact record. Review:
1. Lead source (which form, ad, or channel)
2. Any form data (answers to application questions)
3. Social media profile (if DM lead -- check their bio and recent posts)

> **Decision Point:**
> - If lead submitted the full application form with qualifying data --> Proceed to Step 2 immediately
> - If lead sent a DM with interest signal (asked about program, pricing, or availability) --> Respond within 5 minutes with: "Hey [name]! Thanks for reaching out. I'd love to learn more about your situation. Quick question: what's your biggest fitness challenge right now?" Then proceed to Step 2 when they respond.
> - If lead opted in for lead magnet only (no direct interest signal) --> Tag as "Lead Magnet" in GHL, add to automated nurture sequence "Fitness-Foundations-Nurture". Do NOT call or DM. Move to next lead.
> - If lead source is unclear or data is missing --> Send SMS: "Hey [name], this is Alex from Coach Marcus Rivera's team. Saw you signed up -- just wanted to make sure you got everything you needed. What prompted you to check us out?" Wait for response.

### Step 2: BANT Qualification

**Tool:** Phone (preferred) or DM (if lead came via DM)
**Time:** 5-10 minutes
**When:** Within 5 minutes of identifying a qualified lead

**Action:** Apply the BANT framework in conversational format. Do NOT read questions robotically -- weave them into natural conversation.

**Script framework:**

*Opening (15 seconds):*
"Hey [name], this is Alex from Coach Marcus's team. I saw you [submitted an application / asked about the program / watched the training]. I'd love to learn a bit about your situation to see if we might be a good fit. Do you have about 5 minutes?"

*Budget (B):*
"Have you invested in fitness programs or coaching before? What was that experience like?"
- Score HIGH: Has invested $500+ in fitness programs before
- Score MEDIUM: Willing to invest but has not before ("I've been thinking about getting serious")
- Score LOW: Explicitly price-sensitive ("I'm looking for something affordable") --> Note but continue

*Authority (A):*
"When it comes to making a decision about an investment like this, is that something you'd decide on your own?"
- Score HIGH: Sole decision-maker
- Score MEDIUM: "I'd need to talk to my spouse" --> "Great, would your spouse be able to join the call?"
- Score LOW: Making decision for someone else --> "We'd need [decision-maker] on the call"

*Need (N):*
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how important is it for you to get your fitness handled in the next 90 days?"
- Score HIGH: 8-10 ("It's a priority, I'm ready")
- Score MEDIUM: 5-7 ("It's important but I've been putting it off")
- Score LOW: 1-4 ("Sometime this year maybe")

*Timeline (T):*
"If you found the right program, when would you want to get started?"
- Score HIGH: "Now" or "this month"
- Score MEDIUM: "This quarter"
- Score LOW: "Not sure" or "later this year"

> **Decision Point:**
> - If 3+ of 4 BANT criteria score MEDIUM or higher --> Proceed to Step 3 (Book the Call)
> - If Need scores HIGH (8-10) regardless of other scores --> Proceed to Step 3 (high urgency overrides)
> - If 2+ criteria score LOW --> Do NOT book a call. Say: "I appreciate you sharing that. It sounds like the timing might not be perfect right now. Let me send you some helpful resources in the meantime, and when you're ready to take the next step, you'll know exactly where to find us." Tag as "Nurture-Not-Qualified" in GHL. Add to nurture sequence "Warm-Not-Ready."
> - If lead is hostile, rude, or clearly a bad fit --> Politely end: "Thanks for your time, [name]. I don't think we're the right fit for what you're looking for right now. Best of luck!" Tag as "Disqualified" in GHL.

### Step 3: Book the Call

**Tool:** Calendly (Marcus's closer calendar link)
**Time:** 2 minutes

**Action:**
1. Send Calendly link via SMS and DM: "Great news, [name] -- I think you'd be a perfect fit to chat with Coach Marcus directly. Here's his calendar -- pick a time that works for you: [CALENDLY LINK]. The call is about 30-40 minutes, and Marcus will walk you through exactly how the Metabolic Momentum Method works for your specific situation."
2. If lead books: Update GHL status to "Call Booked." Add note with BANT scores and any relevant context.
3. If lead does not book within 24 hours: Follow up via SMS: "Hey [name], just checking -- did you get a chance to pick a time with Marcus? His calendar fills up fast so I'd grab a spot soon."
4. If lead does not book within 48 hours: Final follow-up: "Hey [name], Marcus has a few spots left this week. Want me to hold one for you? Just let me know and I'll lock it in."
5. If no response after 48-hour follow-up: Tag as "Qualified-No-Book" in GHL. Add to sequence "Re-Engagement-Qualified."

**Confirmation sequence (automated in GHL):**
- Immediately: Booking confirmation email with Zoom link + pre-call questionnaire
- 24 hours before: SMS reminder: "Reminder: Your call with Coach Marcus is tomorrow at [TIME]. Here's your Zoom link: [LINK]"
- 1 hour before: SMS reminder: "Your call with Marcus starts in 1 hour! Here's the link: [LINK]. See you there!"

### Step 4: Pre-Call Brief (Closer -- Marcus)

**Tool:** GHL > Contact Record > Notes tab
**Time:** 5 minutes before each call

**Action:** Marcus reviews before each call:
1. BANT scores and setter notes
2. Pre-call questionnaire responses (if completed)
3. Lead's Instagram/social profile (30 seconds)
4. Any previous interactions or content engagement

**Deliverable:** Mental framework of who this person is, what they need, and which case study best matches their situation.

### Step 5: Sales Call Execution

**Tool:** Zoom
**Time:** 30-45 minutes
**Owner:** Marcus

**Call Structure:**

| Phase | Duration | Focus | Key Actions |
|-------|----------|-------|------------|
| Rapport | 3 min | Build connection | Reference something from their application or social profile |
| Discovery | 10 min | Understand their situation deeply | "Walk me through what your fitness routine looks like right now..." |
| Agitation | 5 min | Surface the cost of inaction | "What happens if nothing changes in the next 12 months?" |
| Mechanism | 8 min | Explain the Metabolic Momentum Method | Use whiteboard or slides, show framework visual |
| Pitch | 7 min | Present the offer | Value stack, bonuses, guarantee, payment options |
| Close | 7 min | Ask for the decision | "Based on everything we've discussed, are you ready to get started?" |
| Admin | 5 min | Process payment or next steps | Stripe link for payment, or schedule follow-up |

> **Decision Point after Close attempt:**
> - "YES" --> Process payment on Stripe. Update GHL to "Closed Won." Trigger onboarding sequence. Celebrate.
> - "I need to think about it" --> "I totally understand. Let me ask -- what specifically do you want to think about? Is it the investment, the timing, or whether this approach is right for you?" Address the real objection. If still no: "How about I check in with you in 48 hours? I'll send you a case study of someone in a similar situation in the meantime." Schedule 48-hour follow-up in GHL.
> - "It's too expensive" --> "I hear you. Let me break down the payment plan option..." Present $1,100 x 3 plan. If still no: "What would make this a no-brainer investment for you?" Listen, address, follow up in 48 hours.
> - "Not now" --> "When would be the right time?" If specific date: schedule follow-up. If vague: "I get it. Let me send you some resources that'll be helpful whenever you're ready." Tag "Qualified-Not-Now" in GHL.
> - "No" (firm) --> "I respect that. Thanks for your time, [name]. If anything changes, you know where to find us." Tag "Closed Lost" with reason in GHL. Add to non-buyer nurture sequence.
> - NO SHOW --> Within 15 minutes: Send SMS: "Hey [name], I was on the Zoom waiting for you! No worries -- things come up. Want to reschedule? Here's the link: [CALENDLY]." If no response in 24 hours: Call directly. If no response in 48 hours: Tag "No Show" and add to re-engagement sequence.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Waiting more than 5 minutes to respond to a new lead.** Prevention: Set phone notifications for GHL new lead alerts. Check smart list every 30 minutes minimum.

2. **Reading the BANT questions like a script.** Prevention: Memorize the framework and practice weaving questions into natural conversation. Record yourself and listen back.

3. **Booking unqualified leads on Marcus's calendar.** Prevention: Apply the BANT scoring strictly. If 2+ criteria are LOW, do not book regardless of how "nice" the lead seems.

4. **Not following up after a no-show.** Prevention: Set automatic task reminders in GHL. No-shows get 3 follow-up attempts before archiving.

5. **Arguing with objections instead of listening.** Prevention: When a prospect raises an objection, pause. Repeat it back: "So what I'm hearing is..." Then address. Never interrupt an objection.

# DM Sequence: Cold DM -- Instagram

## Sequence Meta
- Type: Cold DM
- Platform: Instagram
- Target ICP: Online fitness coaches making $3-8K/month
- Offer: FitPro Accelerator ($1,497)
- Ticket Level: Mid
- Goal: Book a strategy call
- Total Messages: 7 (including 2 follow-ups + graceful exit)
- Timeline: 2-3 weeks

## Pre-Sequence Research Checklist
- [ ] Read their bio: What do they coach? Who do they serve? What's their positioning?
- [ ] Review their last 10-15 posts: What topics do they cover? What language do they use? What are they celebrating or struggling with?
- [ ] Check their follower count and engagement rate (comments vs. likes ratio)
- [ ] Look at their stories: Are they sharing behind-the-scenes? Client wins? Struggles?
- [ ] Identify 2-3 specific personalization hooks (a recent post you genuinely liked, a client result they shared, a question they asked their audience)
- [ ] Check if they follow other business coaches or growth accounts (awareness level signal)
- [ ] Determine their likely awareness level: problem-aware (frustrated but not looking for help) or solution-aware (actively looking for coaching/programs)

## Message 1: Connection [Day 1]

**Timing:** Send between 9am-12pm or 6pm-9pm their local time (highest engagement windows)
**Research required:** Find one specific post, reel, or story to reference genuinely

**Text version:**
```
Hey [PERSONALIZE: first name] -- I saw your
[PERSONALIZE: specific reel/post about specific topic, e.g.,
"reel about progressive overload programming for beginners"]
and I genuinely thought it was one of the better explanations
I've seen on here.

Quick question -- are you finding that your audience engages
with that kind of educational content but it's hard to turn
that engagement into actual paying clients?
```

**Voice message talking points (60 seconds max):**
- "Hey [name], it's [your name]" (5 sec)
- "I just watched your [specific content] and I wanted to tell you [specific thing that impressed you]" (15 sec)
- "I work with a lot of fitness coaches and your content quality is honestly above most of what I see" (10 sec)
- "I was curious -- are you finding it tricky to convert that engagement into paying clients, or is your business side running smoothly?" (15 sec)
- "Either way, just wanted to say great stuff. Talk soon." (10 sec)

**If they respond positively (interested, agree with the question):** -> Message 2A
**If they respond with a question about you:** -> Message 2B
**If they respond briefly ("thanks!" or emoji only):** -> Message 2C
**No response after 72 hours:** -> Follow-Up 1

---

## Message 2A: Curiosity -- Positive Response Branch [Day 2-3]

**Trigger:** They engaged with the question, shared that they do struggle with conversions, or expressed frustration about the engagement-to-client gap.

```
Honestly that's so common -- and it's frustrating because
you're clearly putting out great content.

What does your current setup look like? Like are you mostly
doing 1:1 coaching, group, or a mix?
```

[Coaching: Keep it short. You're showing genuine curiosity,
not interrogating. Their answer tells you their offer
structure, which feeds directly into Message 3.]

**If they share details about their business:** -> Message 3

---

## Message 2B: Curiosity -- Question Response Branch [Day 2-3]

**Trigger:** They asked something like "What do you do?" or "Are you a coach too?" or "Do you work with fitness coaches?"

```
Yeah! I work with online fitness coaches on the business side
-- specifically helping them restructure their offers and
delivery so they can charge premium prices without burning out.

But I'm more curious about you right now -- what does your
coaching business look like at the moment? 1:1, group, mix?
```

[Coaching: Answer their question briefly (2 sentences max)
then redirect back to THEM. The conversation should be 80%
about them, not you.]

**If they share details:** -> Message 3

---

## Message 2C: Curiosity -- Brief Response Branch [Day 3-4]

**Trigger:** They only said "thanks!" or responded with an emoji/brief acknowledgment.

```
For sure! Your content's legit.

Random question -- how long have you been coaching online?
I'm always curious about the journey other coaches are on.
```

[Coaching: Low-pressure follow-up. If they engage with this,
you can progress. If not, move to Follow-Up 1 timing.]

**If they engage:** -> Continue conversation naturally, aiming for Message 3 topics
**If no response after 72h:** -> Follow-Up 1

---

## Message 3: Value + Qualification [Day 5-7]

**Trigger:** You now know something about their business structure (offer type, price point, client count).

```
That makes sense. So you're doing [PERSONALIZE: reflect their
model back, e.g., "1:1 at around $250/month with about 15-20
clients"].

I see that setup a LOT with coaches at your level. The thing
most people don't realize is that the ceiling you hit with
that model isn't about marketing -- it's about the offer
structure itself.

Are you at the point where you're actively trying to figure
out how to break past [PERSONALIZE: their approximate revenue
if shared, or "that plateau"], or is it more of a longer-term
thing?
```

[Coaching: This message does three things:
1. Shows you listened (mirror their model back)
2. Drops a value insight (the problem isn't marketing)
3. Qualifies (are they actively looking for a solution?)]

**If they say they're actively working on it:** -> Message 4A
**If they say it's more long-term:** -> Message 4B
**If no response after 72h:** -> Follow-Up 1

---

## Message 4A: Bridge -- Actively Looking [Day 8-10]

**Trigger:** They indicated they're actively trying to solve this problem.

```
Got it. So here's what I'd actually suggest based on what
you've told me:

The fastest lever most coaches can pull is restructuring
their offer from "monthly coaching at $X" to a specific
transformation for a specific person at $2-5K.

I help fitness coaches do exactly that -- plus redesign their
delivery model so they serve more people in less time.

Would it be helpful to jump on a quick 20-minute call? I can
take a look at your specific situation and give you a game
plan. No pitch, no pressure -- just want to see if I can
help.

I have [PERSONALIZE: day] or [PERSONALIZE: day] open this
week. What works?
```

[Coaching: Notice the structure -- value insight first, then
the bridge to a call. The offer to help comes after you've
already demonstrated understanding of their world.]

**If they book:** -> Confirmation message (below)
**If they hesitate but don't say no:** -> Message 5
**If no response after 72h:** -> Follow-Up 2

---

## Message 4B: Nurture -- Long-Term [Day 8-10]

**Trigger:** They said it's more of a long-term interest, not urgent.

```
Totally get it. No rush at all.

If it's helpful, I actually put together a quick breakdown
of how fitness coaches are restructuring their offers to
charge $3-5K instead of $200-400/month. Want me to send it
over? It's a 5-minute read, no strings.
```

[Coaching: Provide value with zero pressure. If they want
the resource, send it and let the content do the work. Check
back in 2-3 weeks.]

**If they want the resource:** Send it, then follow up in 2-3 weeks
**If no response:** -> Follow-Up 1 (adjusted for nurture)

---

## Message 5: Soft Close [Day 10-12]

**Trigger:** They showed interest in Message 4A but didn't commit to a call.

```
Totally understand if you're slammed. The offer to chat
stands whenever the timing works.

In the meantime -- one of the coaches I work with just went
from $5,200/month to $17,800/month in about 8 weeks. Pretty
similar setup to what you described. Want me to share what
they changed? It's a quick read.
```

[Coaching: Provide value through a case study. This handles the
"does this actually work?" objection without ever pushing for
a call. Let the proof create the pull.]

---

## Follow-Up 1: Re-Engagement [72h after last unanswered message]

```
Hey [PERSONALIZE: first name] -- totally understand if things
are hectic. Just wanted to share something quick:

[PERSONALIZE: choose one based on their situation]

Option A (if they're a fat loss coach): "One of the coaches
I work with in the fat loss space just restructured her offer
and signed 3 clients at $3,500 each in 2 weeks. No ads, no
new followers. Just a better offer and better conversations."

Option B (if they coach athletes): "Saw a college strength
coach go from $300/month to $1,800 per athlete by niching
into off-season protocols. Thought of your situation."

Option C (general): "I just published a breakdown of the 3
things fitness coaches earning $15K+ months do differently.
Want me to send it?"

No pressure at all -- just thought of you.
```

[Coaching: Each follow-up adds new value. Never say "just
following up" or "circling back." Always lead with something
useful.]

---

## Follow-Up 2: Final Value [72h after Follow-Up 1, if still no response]

```
Hey [PERSONALIZE: first name], one more thing and then I'll
leave you be --

I noticed you [PERSONALIZE: reference something current from
their recent content or stories, e.g., "posted about wanting
to take on fewer clients"]. That's actually exactly the kind
of shift the coaches I work with make -- fewer clients, higher
price, better results for everyone.

If that's ever something you want to explore, I'm literally a
message away.
```

[Coaching: This message proves you're still paying attention to
their content -- not just running through a script. It's the
most personalized follow-up and often gets the best response.]

---

## Graceful Exit [If 2 follow-ups go unanswered]

```
Hey [PERSONALIZE: first name] -- no worries at all. I'll
leave the ball in your court from here.

If the business side of coaching ever becomes something you
want to tackle head-on, I'm around. Keep putting out great
content -- your [PERSONALIZE: specific compliment about their
coaching, e.g., "programming breakdowns"] are genuinely
helpful.

Rooting for you!
```

[Coaching: This message does three things:
1. Removes all pressure
2. Leaves the door open
3. Ends on a genuine note that makes them feel good
Never burn a bridge. Some of the best clients come back 3-6
months after a graceful exit.]

---

## Confirmation Message (After They Book a Call)

```
Awesome -- I just sent you a calendar invite for [day] at
[time]. Looking forward to it!

Quick heads up: I'll probably ask you about your current
client count, pricing, and what your ideal month looks like.
Nothing to prepare for -- just come ready to chat honestly
about where things stand.

Talk then!
```

## Platform-Specific Notes (Instagram)
- All messages under 50 words (except Message 4A which is the bridge)
- Voice message option offered at Message 1 -- voice converts 3-5x better for this ICP
- React to their stories 2-3 times before sending Message 1 (warms the connection)
- Never send Message 1 immediately after following -- wait 24-48 hours
- If they post a story relevant to business struggles, use that as a natural DM trigger instead of the cold open
- No links until after they express interest in learning more
- No emojis in every message -- use them sparingly (max 1 per message, and not in every message)

# Sales Script: Closer -- FitPro Accelerator

## Script Meta
- Type: Closer Script
- Duration: 30-40 minutes
- Target ICP: Online fitness coaches making $3-8K/month
- Offer: FitPro Accelerator at $1,497 (3x $547 payment plan)
- Prerequisites: Application form completed, opt-in to VSL training
- Talk Ratio Target: Closer 30% / Prospect 70%

## Pre-Call Checklist
- [ ] Reviewed prospect's application (especially Q6: biggest challenge, Q7: what they've tried)
- [ ] Checked their Instagram profile (following count, content quality, engagement)
- [ ] Identified likely pain points based on their revenue range
- [ ] Selected 2-3 case studies that match their niche and revenue level
- [ ] CRM record updated with pre-call notes

---

## SCRIPT

### RAPPORT [0:00-3:00]

[Coaching: The goal is warmth, frame-setting, and permission to lead.
You have 3 minutes. Do NOT do generic small talk. Reference something
specific from their application.]

"Hey [Name], this is [PERSONALIZE: closer name] from FitPro. Thanks
so much for taking the time today. I've been looking forward to
this because I read through your application and -- I have to say --
your answer about [reference specific application detail, e.g., 'how
you've been coaching for 3 years but feel stuck at the same revenue']
really stood out to me. We hear that a lot from coaches who are
genuinely great at what they do."

**[PAUSE: Let them respond naturally.]**

"Before we dive in, let me tell you how this call works so we
make the best use of your time. I'm going to ask you some questions
to really understand where you're at with your coaching business.
My goal is to figure out if we can genuinely help you. If we can,
I'll show you exactly how. If we can't, I'll tell you that honestly
and point you in the right direction. Either way, you'll leave this
call with clarity. Sound good?"

[Coaching: Wait for their "yes" or "sounds good." This is implicit
permission to control the conversation. If they try to jump ahead
to "just tell me the price," gently redirect: "I want to make sure
this is actually right for you first -- the last thing I want is to
recommend something that doesn't fit your situation."]

"Great. So tell me a little about what's going on with your coaching
business right now."

### DISCOVERY [3:00-18:00]

[Coaching: THIS IS WHERE THE SALE IS MADE OR LOST. Your job is to
listen. Take notes using their EXACT words. You will mirror these
back later. Do not rush through these questions -- let the silence
sit after each one.]

**--- SITUATION QUESTIONS [3:00-6:00] ---**

"Walk me through where things stand right now. How many clients
do you have, and what does your day-to-day look like?"

[Coaching: Listen for: client count, pricing, delivery model, hours
per week, revenue. These are your baseline numbers.]

"Got it. And how long have you been coaching online at this level?"

"What's your current offer structure? Like, what exactly do clients
get when they sign up with you?"

[Coaching: Most will describe an hourly or monthly model. Note exactly
how they describe it -- "I charge $250/month for custom programming
and weekly check-ins" is ammunition for the Premium Positioning
conversation later.]

"And roughly what are you taking home each month after expenses?"

[Coaching: This number matters. If they say $5K revenue but $3.5K
take-home, the gap reveals the real pain. Note it.]

**--- PAIN QUESTIONS [6:00-11:00] ---**

"So here's the big question -- what's the single biggest challenge
you're dealing with right now when it comes to growing?"

[Coaching: Let them talk. Do NOT interrupt. Whatever they say first
is what's top of mind -- note it word-for-word.]

"Help me understand that a little more. When you say
'[THEIR EXACT WORDS]' -- what does that actually look like on a
typical week?"

[Coaching: Go deeper. Generic answers like "I just need more clients"
are surface-level. Push for specifics: "What does 'need more clients'
actually mean? Like, are you losing clients faster than you're
gaining them? Or are you at capacity and can't fit more in?"]

"And how is this affecting you personally? Not just the business
numbers, but you -- your energy, your motivation, your life outside
of coaching?"

[Coaching: This question often unlocks the emotional truth. Listen
for: burnout, relationship strain, passion fading, identity crisis.
These are the real drivers of the decision.]

"Let me ask you something that might be uncomfortable but it's
important. If you had to put a number on it -- what has staying
stuck at this level cost you over the last 6-12 months? In revenue
you could have earned, in time you've spent that wasn't productive,
in opportunities you've missed?"

[Coaching: Make them quantify it. If they say "probably $30-40K in
revenue I left on the table" -- that becomes your price anchor later.
If they struggle to quantify, help: "Well, if you could have been
at $15K/month instead of $6K, that's $108K over a year. Even half
of that is $54K. Does that feel about right?"]

"And what happens if you don't solve this? Like, if we're having
this same conversation a year from now and nothing has changed --
what does that look like for you?"

**[PAUSE: 5 seconds. Let the weight of that land.]**

[Coaching: This is the most important question on the call. Their
answer to "what happens if nothing changes" creates the urgency.
You don't need to create urgency -- they just created it themselves.]

**--- DESIRE QUESTIONS [11:00-14:00] ---**

"Okay, so let's flip it. If you could wave a magic wand and have
your coaching business exactly where you want it -- what does that
look like?"

[Coaching: Listen for specific numbers (revenue, clients, hours) and
emotional desires (freedom, confidence, fulfillment). Note both.]

"What would change in your life -- not just your business -- if
you had that?"

"And what's your timeline? When do you want to be at that level?"

"Here's the question that matters most: what triggered you to watch
the training and apply right now? Why not 6 months ago or 6 months
from now?"

[Coaching: Their "why now" reveals urgency level. Strong triggers:
"I just lost 3 clients," "I'm about to have a baby and need to
work less," "I hit my ceiling again this month." Weak trigger: "I
just saw the ad." Adapt your urgency accordingly.]

**--- DECISION QUESTIONS [14:00-18:00] ---**

"Have you ever invested in business coaching or a program before?"

[If yes:] "What was that experience like? What worked and what didn't?"

[Coaching: If they had a bad experience, acknowledge it directly:
"So what you need this time is something fundamentally different
from what you tried before. Would you agree?" This pre-handles the
"I've been burned" objection.]

[If no:] "What's held you back from investing in help until now?"

[Coaching: Usually fear of wasting money or belief they should
figure it out themselves. Note it -- these are objections to address.]

"Is there anyone else involved in this decision? A partner, spouse,
business partner?"

[If yes:] "Got it. Would it be helpful to have them on a call too,
or do you usually make business decisions and then fill them in?"

"Last question before I share some thoughts. If I could show you a
clear path from where you are now at $[THEIR NUMBER] to $[THEIR
GOAL NUMBER], and you felt genuinely confident it would work for
your specific situation -- is there anything that would hold you
back from getting started?"

[Coaching: This is the isolation question. Whatever they say is the
real objection. If they say "nothing" -- they're pre-closing
themselves. If they say "the money" or "the timing" -- note it and
address it before the pitch. If they name something specific,
handle it now: "Let me address that right now before we go any
further..."]

### PRESCRIPTION [18:00-21:00]

[Coaching: Mirror their exact words back. This creates deep trust
and proves you were genuinely listening. If you get even one word
wrong, they'll correct you -- which is fine, it shows you care
about accuracy.]

"Okay [Name], let me make sure I have this right."

"You're currently at about $[THEIR REVENUE] a month with [THEIR
CLIENT COUNT] clients, charging $[THEIR PRICE] per client. You're
working about [THEIR HOURS] hours a week, and your take-home after
expenses is around $[THEIR TAKE-HOME]."

"The biggest challenge you're facing is [THEIR EXACT WORDS ABOUT
THEIR CHALLENGE]. And that's cost you roughly $[THEIR ESTIMATE] over
the last [THEIR TIMEFRAME]."

"Ideally, you want to be at $[THEIR GOAL REVENUE] a month, working
[THEIR GOAL HOURS] hours or less, within [THEIR TIMELINE]. And the
reason you're looking at this now is [THEIR TRIGGER]."

"Is that accurate?"

[Coaching: Wait for confirmation. If they add or correct, update
your notes. Then:]

"Based on everything you've shared, I genuinely believe we can help.
Would you be open to me showing you how?"

[Coaching: Do NOT skip the permission question. Launching into the
pitch without permission creates subconscious resistance.]

### PRESENTATION [21:00-30:00]

[Coaching: Every element you present must connect to something they
said in discovery. Do not present generic features.]

"So here's what we do at FitPro. We take coaches who are exactly
where you are -- making $[THEIR NUMBER], working [THEIR HOURS] hours,
feeling [THEIR EMOTIONAL STATE] -- and we help them get to
$[THEIR GOAL] using something we call the FitPro Method."

"The reason it works, and the reason it's different from [what they
tried before OR generic business coaching], is that it's built
specifically for fitness coaches. It addresses the three actual
bottlenecks that keep coaches stuck."

**Pillar 1: Premium Positioning**
"First, we rebuild your offer. You mentioned you're charging
$[THEIR PRICE] for [THEIR OFFER DESCRIPTION]. The challenge with
that model is [specific limitation tied to their pain]. What we do
is help you create a premium transformation offer -- something like
a $2,000-$5,000 package that a specific type of client will happily
pay for. Because here's the truth: your expertise is worth way more
than $[THEIR PRICE]/month. You just haven't packaged it that way yet."

**Pillar 2: Leveraged Delivery**
"Second -- and this addresses exactly what you said about
[THEIR CONCERN ABOUT HOURS/CAPACITY] -- we redesign how you deliver.
Instead of [their current delivery model], we implement a hybrid
system that lets you serve more clients in fewer hours while they
actually get better results. I know that sounds counterintuitive,
but [case study name] did exactly this and went from [hours] to
[hours] while their retention rate went up."

**Pillar 3: Conversion Engine**
"Third, we install a conversion system. Not more content. Not ads.
A system for turning the people who already follow you into
qualified conversations. You mentioned you have [their audience size
or engagement]. That's more than enough -- you just need the right
system for having the right conversations."

**Social Proof:**
"Let me give you an example. [Case study name] came to us in a
really similar situation to yours -- [brief parallel]. Within
[timeframe], they [specific result]. And they're a [their niche]
coach, so it's a pretty direct comparison to what you do."

**What's included:**
"So here's specifically what you'd get: [walk through key components,
connecting each to their specific pain points. Do NOT read a list --
make each component a response to something they said]."

**Guarantee:**
"And here's our guarantee: go through the full 12-week program,
implement the three pillars, show up to the calls. If you haven't
added at least $3,000 in monthly revenue, we'll coach you for
another 12 weeks at no charge. The risk is on us."

### CLOSE [30:00-34:00]

[Coaching: State the price with total confidence. No hedging, no
qualifying, no "it's only" or "it's just." State it and STOP.]

"So the investment for the FitPro Accelerator is $1,497."

**[SILENCE. Count to 10 in your head. Do NOT fill the silence. The
first person to speak after the price loses leverage.]**

[If they ask about payment plans:]
"Yeah, absolutely. We have a 3-payment plan of $547 per month.
Would that work better for your situation?"

[If they're ready:]
"Awesome. Here's how we get you started. I'm going to send you an
enrollment link right now. Once you complete enrollment, you'll get
immediate access to the Premium Positioning Workshop -- which is
where most coaches see their first breakthrough -- and your first
group coaching call is [date]. Sound good?"

[If they hesitate or say they need to think about it -- move to
objection handling.]

### OBJECTION HANDLING

**--- "I need to think about it" ---**

[Coaching: This is NEVER the real objection. Something specific is
unresolved.]

"I completely respect that -- this is a real decision and I want
you to make the right one. Can I ask you something though? When
you say you need to think about it, is it more about the money,
the timing, or whether you believe this is the right approach for
your specific situation?"

[Wait for their answer. That's the real objection. Address THAT.]

---

**--- "It's too expensive" / "I can't afford it right now" ---**

"I hear you. Money's a real consideration. Let me ask you this --
you mentioned that staying at $[THEIR REVENUE] has cost you roughly
$[THEIR ESTIMATE] over the past year. If the FitPro Method helps
you add even $3,000 a month -- which is our minimum guarantee --
that's $36,000 over the next year on a $1,497 investment. That's
a 24:1 return."

**[PAUSE]**

"And we do have the 3-payment plan at $547/month. When you think
about it, that's less than the revenue from one premium client
at the new pricing we'd help you set up. Most coaches sign their
first premium client within weeks 3-4. Would the payment plan make
this workable?"

---

**--- "I've tried programs before that didn't work" ---**

"I appreciate you being honest about that. Can I ask -- what
specifically didn't work about [what they tried]?"

[Listen. Then:]

"Here's what I'm hearing -- [summarize what went wrong]. That
makes complete sense. And honestly, that's exactly why the FitPro
Accelerator exists. What you experienced was [wrong fit / generic
advice / no accountability / no implementation support]. We're
different because [specific differentiation]. And [case study name]
actually had the exact same experience with [similar program] before
joining us -- and they went on to [result]."

---

**--- "I need to talk to my wife/partner" ---**

"Absolutely -- that's important. And I respect that you make these
decisions together. Here's what I'd suggest: would it be helpful
if I sent you a summary of everything we discussed, including the
case studies and the guarantee, so you can walk them through it?
Or, if it would be easier, I'm happy to jump on a quick 10-minute
call with both of you."

---

**--- "My niche is different / I'm not sure this applies to me" ---**

"Great question. What specifically about your niche do you think
might make this different?"

[Listen. Then:]

"That actually makes sense. And here's the thing -- the FitPro
Method is built on business fundamentals: offer structure, delivery
design, and conversion systems. These work across every fitness
niche we've seen. We've had coaches in [list relevant niches].
The system adapts to your niche -- it doesn't require your niche
to adapt to it."

---

**--- POST-OBJECTION RE-CLOSE ---**

"Does that address your concern?"

[Wait for yes.]

"Great. So if that piece is handled, is there anything else
holding you back from getting started?"

[If nothing:] "Awesome. Let me send you the enrollment link
right now."

### POST-CALL PROTOCOL

**If Closed:**
1. Send enrollment link immediately (within 5 minutes)
2. Update CRM: Stage -> Closed Won, amount, payment type, notes
3. Send welcome email from creator (automated trigger)
4. Notify fulfillment team
5. Add to onboarding sequence
6. Schedule Day 3 check-in call

**If Follow-Up Needed:**
1. Confirm specific follow-up date and time
2. Send summary email within 1 hour:
   - Recap of their situation (in their words)
   - How the program addresses their specific challenges
   - 1-2 relevant case studies
   - Guarantee reminder
   - "Looking forward to connecting on [date]"
3. Update CRM: Stage -> Follow-Up, follow-up date, blocking objection
4. Set reminder for follow-up

**If Lost:**
1. Update CRM: Stage -> Closed Lost, reason (required), notes
2. Send graceful close email within 1 hour:
   - "Thanks for the conversation"
   - Offer a free resource relevant to their challenge
   - "Door is always open"
3. Move to long-term nurture sequence
4. Log objections for team learning

## Contingency Plans

### Scenario A: Below 30% of target at mid-launch (Day 4: <8 sales)

**Actions:**
1. Add fast-action bonus: "Apply in the next 48 hours and get a 1-on-1 Metabolic Assessment call ($500 value)" -- Marcus, announce by 6pm Day 4
2. Schedule emergency IG Live for Day 5 at 8pm: "Ask Marcus Anything about FitFoundation" -- Marcus
3. Expand DM outreach: Alex contacts an additional 75 leads from the comment/engagement list -- Alex, Days 5-6
4. Increase ad spend from $500/day to $800/day on best-performing retarget set -- VA, immediately
5. Marcus records 60-second personal video addressing the #1 objection ("I don't have time") and posts as reel -- Marcus, Day 5

### Scenario B: Technical failure (payment link down, email not sending)

**Actions:**
1. Backup payment link: manual Stripe invoice (template pre-built) -- VA, within 15 minutes
2. Backup email: send via personal Gmail to active applicants with apology + direct link -- Marcus, within 30 minutes
3. Communication: Post IG story "We're having a small tech hiccup -- here's the direct link: [URL]" -- Marcus, immediately
4. Extend cart by the number of hours the system was down -- Marcus, decision within 2 hours

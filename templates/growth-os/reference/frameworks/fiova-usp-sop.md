# FIOVA USP Creation SOP

> FIOVA domain knowledge — used by Foundation agents for market research,
> ICP building, and offer architecture. All methodology is FIOVA-branded.

---

\t**Ultimate SOP for Crafting a Direct, Irresistible USP**
\t**Purpose:**
\tTo give founders, marketers, and copywriters a repeatable system to create a Unique Selling Proposition that *pierces through market noise*, converts cold traffic, and fuels all downstream messaging—ads, VSLs, email, sales calls, content.
\t---
\t**PHASE 1: MINDSET & STRATEGY (Borrowed from Eugene Schwartz, Jeremy Haynes, Hormozi)**
\t1. **Start with Stage of Awareness**
\t\t- Problem Aware: They know the pain but not the solution
\t\t- Solution Aware: They’ve tried other programs or heard similar claims
\t\t- Product Aware: They’ve seen your funnel or content before
\t2. *Tailor the USP to match their skepticism and belief level.*
\t3. **Know the Market Sophistication (Schwartz’s 5 Levels)**
\t\t- **Stage 1**: Bold promise
\t\t- **Stage 2**: Introduce mechanism
\t\t- **Stage 3**: Superior mechanism
\t\t- **Stage 4**: Emotion, identity, outcome-driven
\t\t- **Stage 5**: Proof-heavy, story-led, belief-breaking
\t4. **One Clear Target Avatar**
\t\t- Who is the *exact* person this offer is for?
\t\t- What result do they deeply desire?
\t\t- What pain do they silently suffer with?
\t\t- What belief do they hold that keeps them stuck?
\t5. **What’s Their Nightmare? What’s Their Dream?**
\t\t- Ex: “Working 12 hours/day and still not breaking \\$10K/mo”
\t\t- Ex: “Imagine scaling to \\$100K/mo while taking Fridays off”
\t---
\t**PHASE 2: BUILDING BLOCKS OF A DIRECT USP**
\tEvery winning USP includes these 5 core parts:
\t1. **The Avatar (Who’s it for?)**
\t\t- “For coaches stuck under \\$30K/mo…”
\t2. **The Desired Outcome (What do they want most?)**
\t\t- “…who want to scale to \\$100K/mo without hiring 5 setters…”
\t3. **Unique Mechanism (What makes you different?)**
\t\t- “…using our AI Operator System that replaces 3 team members.”
\t4. **Timeframe or Urgency (Why now?)**
\t\t- “…in 6 weeks or less with same-day implementation.”
\t5. **Big Emotional Benefit (What does life feel like after?)**
\t\t- “…so you can finally stop managing people and start printing money.”
\t---
\t**PHASE 3: FORMULAS TO FOLLOW**
\tUse these to punch out draft USP lines:
\t**Formula 1: Hormozi-Style**
\t“We help \\[Avatar\\] get \\[Result\\] without \\[Pain\\] using \\[Unique Mechanism\\] in \\[Timeframe\\].”
\t**Formula 2: Jeremy Haynes Punchline**
\t“This is the only \\[Category\\] that \\[Bold Outcome\\] *even if* \\[Skeptical Objection\\].”
\t**Formula 3: Market Domination Framework**
\t“If you’re a \\[Niche Avatar\\] and you’re not using \\[Your Method\\], you’re already falling behind.”
\t**Formula 4: Anti-Vanity Play (Disqualify + Clarify)**
\t“Not for beginners. This is for seasoned \\[Avatar\\] who are sick of \\[Pain\\] and ready for \\[Outcome\\]—done our way.”
\t---
\t**PHASE 4: TESTING & REFINEMENT**
\t1. **Say it out loud** → Does it feel real or hypey?
\t2. **Post it in your story as a poll** → What CTA or comment feedback do you get?
\t3. **Run a cold DM test** → Drop the USP raw and see if anyone replies with interest.
\t4. **Ask: Would I bet \\$100K this line can beat a control ad?**
\t---
\t**PHASE 5: DEPLOY IT EVERYWHERE**
\tOnce dialed, your USP becomes the *engine*:
\t- Ad Primary Text
\t- VSL Opening Line
\t- Instagram Bio
\t- Email Subject Line
\t- Discovery Call Opener
\t- Funnel Headline
\t- Referral Talking Point
\t---
\t**Pro Tip: USP ≠ Tagline**
\tYour USP is not just a clever line. It’s a *strategic claim* that drives alignment across every piece of the business.
\t---
\t**Examples:**
\t✅ “We help online fitness coaches hit \\$100K/mo using a 3-Phase Retention System that keeps clients paying 3x longer.”
\t✅ “This is the only coaching program built to replace setters and sales managers with AI workflows—so you can coach and collect.”
\t✅ “Not for beginners. This is for coaches doing \\$20–50K/mo who want to escape their Slack channel and still grow 2x.”
\t---
\t**Final Reminder:** If you can’t say it in one sentence, it’s not a real USP. Clarity wins. Boldness converts. Specificity scales.

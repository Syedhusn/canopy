# CUSTOMER BUBBLE — SALES TEAM STUDY GUIDE
## Drysdale Inner Circle 2.0

---

## WHAT IS THE CUSTOMER BUBBLE?

**The customer bubble is the reality your prospect lives in—what they believe, want, fear, and suspect.**
Your job as a closer/setter is to **enter their bubble** and speak their language.

### The Golden Rule:
> "People will do anything for someone who encourages their dreams, confirms their suspicions, allays their fears, justifies their failures, and throws rocks at their enemies."

---

## WHO IS OUR IDEAL CUSTOMER?

**Demographics:**
- Age: 35-55 years old
- Experience: 1-3 years trading (some 6 months, some 5+ years)
- Status: Unprofitable Struggling or break-even trader
- Family: Often married with 3-5 kids
- Employment: Full-time job they want to escape OR recently laid off

**Trading Experience:**
- Blown 2-5+ funded accounts
- Passed challenges but can't keep accounts
- Tried 3-5+ different strategies (ICT, SMC, order blocks)
- Spent $5K-15K+ on courses/mentorships
- Consumed hundreds of hours of YouTube content
- In multiple Discord groups (500+ members where they're invisible)

---

## THE 5 COMPONENTS OF THE CUSTOMER BUBBLE

---

### 1. BELIEFS (What They Think Is True)

**About Trading:**
- "Trading can create financial freedom" (they've seen proof)
- "I need a proven system to succeed"
- "Psychology is a major factor"
- "I just need one more course and it'll click"
- "If I work harder and study more, I'll figure it out"
- "Other people can do it, but maybe I'm not cut out for this"
- "All mentorships are scams"

**About Themselves:**
- "I'm capable, I just need the right guidance"
- "I'm not disciplined enough" — **THIS IS THE BIG ONE**
- "I'm too old/too late to succeed"
- "Everyone else gets it except me"

**About Success:**
- "Accountability and mentorship accelerate results"
- "I can figure this out on my own"
- "Success in trading takes 5-10 years"

---

### 2. DESIRES (What They Want)

**What They SAY They Want:**
- Consistent funded account payouts within 90 days
- $4K-10K monthly income from trading
- Replace or supplement current income ($80K-120K annually)
- Discipline and confidence in execution
- Clear, mechanical trading system
- Structured mentorship with accountability

**What They REALLY Want (Deeper):**
- **Identity transformation** — From "struggling trader" to "professional trader"
- **Validation** — Prove to themselves (and others) they're not a failure
- **Escape** — Get away from soul-crushing job, financial pressure, time poverty
- **Respect** — Prove doubters wrong (spouse, friends, themselves)
- **End the cycle** — Break free from years of "almost" and failure

**What They DON'T Want (But Won't Say):**
- Another course that doesn't deliver
- To waste more money and time
- To feel stupid or incompetent
- To be in a massive Discord where they're ignored

---

### 3. EMOTIONS (How They Feel)

**Fear (95% confidence):**
- Fear of losing funded accounts again
- Fear of wasting more money on mentorship
- Fear of never succeeding at trading
- Fear of disappointing family
- Fear of clicking the button on trades

**Frustration (93% confidence):**
- Frustrated by inconsistent results
- Frustrated by conflicting information
- Frustrated by lack of progress despite effort

**Shame (88% confidence):**
- Shame of being break-even after years
- Shame of blown accounts
- Shame of spending money on courses that didn't work

**Hope (90% confidence):**
- Hope that THIS time it'll be different
- Hope that Chris's system is the answer
- Hope that 2026 can be their year

**Desperation (85% confidence):**
- Desperate for something to work
- Desperate to escape current situation
- Desperate for accountability and structure

**Isolation (87% confidence):**
- Feeling alone in their struggles
- No one to talk to who understands
- Lost in massive communities

---

### 4. PAINS & FAILURES (What They've Struggled With)

**Trading Execution Failures:**
- Blown multiple funded accounts (2-5+ times)
- Passed challenges but can't keep accounts
- Revenge trading after losses
- Cutting winners early, letting losers run
- **Freezing when it's time to execute** — **BIG ONE**
- Getting tentative when account grows
- Paper cuts bleeding accounts

**Learning & System Failures:**
- Tried 3-5+ different strategies
- Jumped between systems without mastering one
- Consumed hundreds of hours of content
- Joined multiple Discord groups
- Bought multiple courses ($3K-10K+ spent)

**Psychological Failures:**
- Can't stick to trade plan
- Lack of discipline in journaling
- Emotional decision-making
- **Second-guessing every setup** — **BIG ONE**
- Lack of confidence in execution

**Accountability Failures:**
- Tried trading alone without structure
- Started journaling but quit after 2 weeks
- Promised spouse they'd succeed but didn't

---

### 5. SUSPICIONS (What They Doubt & Don't Trust)

**About Mentorships:**
- "This is probably another scam"
- "They'll take my money and ghost me"
- "It's just another course with no real support"
- "The guarantee probably has fine print"
- "I won't get direct access to Chris, just assistants"

**About Themselves:**
- "Maybe I'm not cut out for trading"
- "Maybe I'm not disciplined enough"

**About Investment:**
- "I can't afford $5,000 right now"
- "My spouse will be mad if I spend more money"
- "What if this doesn't work either?"

---

## HOW TO USE THIS ON SALES CALLS

---

### 1. ENCOURAGE THEIR DREAMS

**What This Means:** Support their vision of becoming consistently profitable.

**What To Say:**
- "You're in the top 1% of funded traders just by passing accounts"
- "You have the potential, you just need the right system and accountability"
- "2026 can be the year everything changes for you"
- "Imagine waking up every day with complete confidence in your trading plan"

---

### 2. CONFIRM THEIR SUSPICIONS

**What This Means:** Validate what they already suspect is true.

**What To Say:**
- "You've been burned before by mentorships, we get it"
- "You're struggling because of psychology, not strategy—psychology is 80% of trading"
- "Massive Discord groups are useless—that's why we cap at 15-20 traders"
- "You know you have the potential—something's just been holding you back"

---

### 3. ALLAY THEIR FEARS

**What This Means:** Remove their fears directly.

**What To Say:**
- "90-day payout guarantee—if you don't hit a payout, you get free coaching until you do. Zero risk."
- "You get direct access to Chris, not just assistants"
- "Small group (15-20 max)—Chris knows your name and tracks your patterns"
- "Payment plans available—one payout covers the entire investment"

---

### 4. JUSTIFY THEIR FAILURES

**What This Means:** Show them it's not their fault.

**What To Say:**
- "It's not that you're not disciplined enough"
- "The problem is you're trying to execute a discretionary strategy where every trader interprets setups differently—so you second-guess every entry"
- "ICT has 50+ confluences. Takes years to master. That's why you freeze."
- "Michael was stuck for 3-5 years. PDC Wizard blew multiple accounts. It's not just you."

---

### 5. THROW ROCKS AT THEIR ENEMIES

**What This Means:** Attack what's been holding them back.

**What To Say:**
- "ICT has 50+ confluences. Takes years to master."
- "SMC gives you analysis paralysis. You see setups everywhere but can't pull the trigger."
- "Order blocks are subjective. Every trader interprets them differently."
- "Massive Discord groups (500+ members) where nobody knows your name—you're invisible."
- "Courses that leave you alone to figure it out—no accountability, no support."

---

## THEIR EXACT LANGUAGE (USE THIS IN CALLS)

These are the EXACT phrases prospects use on sales calls. **Mirror their language back to them.**

- "I keep bleeding my account down with paper cuts."
- "I know what to do but I can't execute when it matters."
- "I get tentative when my account grows."
- "I freeze on A+ setups, then revenge trade trying to make it back."
- "I've been doing this for 2 years and I'm still not consistent. What's wrong with me?"
- "I second-guess every entry."
- "I've spent $5K-10K on courses and I'm still break-even."
- "My wife doesn't understand why I keep losing money on these courses."
- "I'm tired of massive Discord groups where nobody knows my name."

---

## REMEMBER:

1. **Enter their bubble** — Speak their language, not marketing speak
2. **Use their exact phrases** — "I freeze on A+ setups" not "You struggle with execution"
3. **Validate their struggle** — "It's not your fault"
4. **Show them the new path** — Mechanical system + Daily accountability
5. **Remove risk** — 90-day guarantee, payment plans, small group

---

**Study this. Know this. Live this. This is how you close deals.**

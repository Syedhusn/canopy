\t\tHelping personal trainers *(in-person, hybrid, or early-stage online) *or 9-5 workers who want to sustainably scale to \\$10K/month online doing coaching that actually changes lives — without relying on guesswork, generic content, or burning out.
\t\t### **What is their current situation? (go into intricate details)**
\t\t- They’re either still on the gym floor, doing some freelance PT, or dabbling in online coaching with a few clients — but it’s not consistent.
\t\t- They’ve maybe made £1–2K online before, had a fluke £3–5K month, or are stuck around £500–£2K per month with zero predictability.
\t\t- They’re second-guessing if online coaching *actually works*. They’re unsure whether clients even get results without in-person sessions.
\t\t- They’re bouncing between strategies — posting content that gets likes but no clients, getting ghosted in the DMs, and fumbling on sales calls.
\t\t- They feel like they’re building someone else’s dream — working in gyms, part-time jobs, or side hustles just to stay afloat. They want to start online but are hesitant to take the step.
\t\t- They’ve got the potential but no clear system for audience building, lead flow, conversion, retention, or client delivery at scale.
\t\t### **What do they want? (go into intricate detail)**
\t\t- To earn £5–10K/month consistently online doing what they love — not stuck cleaning gym equipment or being undervalued.
\t\t- To be able to quit their job, travel the world, and coach from anywhere while having real impact.
\t\t- To wake up with a calendar full of coaching calls or check-ins, to know they’re making progress *and* money.
\t\t- To feel proud that they own a business that’s theirs — no boss, no limitations, full autonomy.
\t\t- To be seen as an authority — not just another online coach, but someone with real results, client impact, and a growing brand.
\t\t- They want clients reaching out to them through organic content without spending a penny on ads.
\t\t### **What kind of work life do they have?**
\t\t- It’s fragmented — they’re either overworked doing gym shifts + coaching on the side, or they’ve gone all-in but have no income stability.
\t\t- They’re on their phones all day, either posting and praying or stuck in endless learning loops (courses, PDFs, YouTubes) but not getting clients.
\t\t- Their routine is chaotic — they’re not clear on what to focus on, how to attract leads, or how to build a real business backend.
\t\t- They don’t have a system for client fulfillment that’s scalable — they feel like they’re winging it every time a new client signs up.
\t\t### **What are their relationships like?**
\t\t- Friends/family don’t understand what they’re doing or why they’re taking the risk — they feel isolated.
\t\t- Romantic relationships might feel strained — especially if money is tight or their partner doesn’t believe in the “online thing.”
\t\t- They’re surrounded by gym bros or trainers who gave up — which makes them feel doubt, especially during tough weeks.
\t\t- They want to connect with other high-performers who actually *get* the mission — building a business that transforms lives.

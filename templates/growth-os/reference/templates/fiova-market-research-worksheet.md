\t# Market Research Template
\t# **Step 1.1: Avatar Deep Dive**
\t**1.** What is the biggest result you can help a business/person achieve?
\t**2.** Describe your most ideal client/customer in rich detail (age, gender, lifestyle, job, family, hobbies). Do they use a specific name to describe themselves?
\t**3.** What’s the biggest problem your most ideal customer has?
\t**4.** What frustrates your ideal customer the most?
\t**5.** What keeps your ideal customer awake at night?
\t**6.** What humiliates your perfect client (an event or occurrence they’re trying to avoid)?
\t**7.** What’s the cost of staying where they are right now? How bad can things get if they don’t fix it?
\t**8.** What is their most urgent, pressing crisis they want solved right away?
\t**9.** What are the top 3 things that frustrate your perfect clients on a daily basis?
\t**10.** What does your perfect client complain about when they're with their friends or family?
\t**11.** What does your most ideal client/customer want more than anything else?
\t**12.** What is the biggest mistake your perfect client is making right now (related to the problem you solve)?
\t# **Step 1.2: Schwartz market awareness level.**
\tDeeply explain the exact market awareness levels that our mentorship offer is going to target.
\t# **Step 1.3: Unique Mechanism**
\t### **Unique Mechanism Analysis**
\t1. **What is the core unique mechanism?**
\t\t(E.g., a specific method, framework, formula, or process mentioned in the VSL or content.)
\t2. **How does the creator describe how it works?**
\t\t(What are the steps, ingredients, or moving parts?)
\t3. **Why does the creator claim it works better than competitors’ approaches?**
\t\t(What common industry methods or misconceptions does it challenge?)
\t4. **What type of mechanism is it?**
\t\t(Choose: Method / System / Framework / Formula / Tool / Algorithm / Structure / Process.)
\t5. **What belief shift does this mechanism create for the avatar?**
\t\t(What do they stop believing about the old way? What new belief replaces it?)
\t6. **What tangible proof supports it?**
\t\t(Client results, screenshots, testimonials, before-after metrics, etc.)
\t7. **What emotional proof supports it?**
\t\t(Stories, transformations, identity-level changes, student feelings, etc.)
\t8. **What metaphors or examples are used to explain it?**
\t\t(E.g., “Like flipping a switch,” “Think of it like a conveyor belt,” etc.)
\t9. **What is the mechanism’s *superpower*?**
\t\t(The one thing that makes it instantly understandable and desirable.)
\t10. **What potential weaknesses or points of skepticism could prospects have about it?**
\t\t(And how does the creator handle those?)

\t# **Step 2: Offer Positioning**
\t**13.** Who are you, what do you do, and who do you help exactly?
\t**14.** What are some of your biggest accomplishments, awards, or features?
\t**15.** What are the 3–5 things they need to do to fix their problem or achieve their desired result?
\t**16.** What are you offering to them to help them in your ad exactly? (Free training, free strategy call, etc.)
\t**17.** What is the unique mechanism that makes your offer different? Why is it better than what they’ve already tried?
\t**18.** What do they get when they click your ad? What are the immediate benefits?
\t**19.** Do you offer a no-risk guarantee? If so, what is it (1–2 sentences max)?
\t**20.** Why should they get what you have to offer now and not wait? What is the cost of not taking action?
